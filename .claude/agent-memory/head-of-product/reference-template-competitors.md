**User-named (initial set):** supastarter, supafast, nextforge.

**Verified in research pass 2026-06-16** (see [[project-market-research-2026-06]] for full data):

| Name | Price | Stack | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| supastarter | €349/€799/€1,499 | Next.js | Direct competitor |
| Makerkit | $299/$599 | Next.js (3 parallel stacks) | Direct competitor |
| ShipFast | $199/$249/$299 | Next.js | Direct competitor (distribution outlier) |
| TurboStarter | $249/$399 | Next.js (multi-app) | Direct competitor |
| SaasRock | $249/$499/$1,999 | **Remix/RR7** | Adjacent (not Next.js) — deep RBAC |
| Nexty (nextforge) | $188 | Next.js | Below price band, multi-modal demos |

**Non-findings** (treat as not-competitors):
- mrrobot — no paid product in band
- supafast.co — unreliable, no verified claims

**Adjacent / free floor to track:**
- create-t3-app (free — "what people build for free" baseline)
- vercel's own starters
- launchui / shadcn templates (component-level, not full apps)

**How to use:** When pricing, building, or writing positioning, score yourself against this set on (RAG, vector DB, agents, observability, evals, cost tracking, structured output). The current gap is end-user AI depth — see [[project-market-research-2026-06]].
